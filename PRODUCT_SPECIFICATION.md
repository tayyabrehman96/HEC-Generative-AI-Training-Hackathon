## Product scope (summary)

**Sehat Saathi** is a patient-facing assistant that interprets **photos of Pakistani prescriptions** (including difficult handwriting and shorthand) and presents **structured, Urdu-accessible explanations**: purpose, usage, timing, schedule decoding, alternatives, indicative **PKR** cost context, optional **text-to-speech**, and educational **interaction hints** when multiple medicines are present. The system does **not** replace licensed medical or pharmacy advice.
